Frequently Asked Questions

What types of facilities do you serve?

We provide on-site podiatric care for skilled nursing facilities (SNFs), assisted living facilities (ALFs), rehabilitation centers, long-term care communities, memory care, and hospice settings throughout Miami-Dade and Broward counties.

What services do you provide on-site?

Our services include comprehensive foot and ankle evaluations, diabetic foot care, advanced wound care, limb preservation, routine foot care, pressure injury prevention, bedside minor procedures, infection management, vascular and neuropathy assessments, and treatment of foot and ankle pain.

How quickly can residents be seen, and how often do you visit?

We work closely with each facility to establish a consistent visitation schedule based on resident needs and census. We also prioritize timely evaluations for urgent concerns, ensuring residents receive prompt, ongoing care without unnecessary delays.

Does your team handle documentation and communication?

Yes. We complete real-time bedside documentation and communicate treatment plans, recommendations, and follow-up needs directly with facility staff, helping improve coordination of care and supporting quality metrics.

How do you ensure hygiene and safety during visits?

We follow strict sterilization and infection control protocols during every visit. All equipment is thoroughly sanitized, and our providers adhere to the highest standards of cleanliness and safety to protect residents, staff, and the facility environment.

How does partnering with Precision Podiatry Group benefit our facility?

Our proactive approach helps identify problems before they become emergencies, reducing avoidable hospitalizations, supporting limb preservation, improving resident mobility, enhancing quality outcomes, and providing families with greater peace of mind—all while reducing the burden on your clinical staff.
